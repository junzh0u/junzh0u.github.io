Uses

The gear and software I actually use

Hardware

One main machine and a handful of peripherals I’ve stuck with for years.

  • MacBook Pro (M1 Max, 64 GB) — compiles, VMs, and local models without breaking a sweat
  • Apple Studio Display (1st gen)
  • NuPhy Halo75 — low-profile mechanical keyboard
  • Logitech G502 Lightspeed + PowerPlay — the mouse I never have to charge
  • iPad Pro 12.9″ (5th gen) — reading, notes, second screen

Software

A terminal-first setup I keep filing down — including a couple of tools I maintain myself.

  • zsh
  • starship — prompt; I run my own fork
  • Ghostty — terminal
  • fzf — fuzzy-find everything
  • mvx — my own mv/cp replacement

Gaming

Console, handheld, headset, and a compact PC — whatever fits the mood.

  • Sony PS5 — the couch console
  • Nintendo Switch 2 — handheld for everywhere
  • Meta Quest 3 — VR, when a flat screen won’t do
  • Beelink GTi14 Ultra (Core Ultra 9 185H) + EX Dock — the gaming PC; GPU in the EX dock

Office

You spend all day here, so it’s worth getting right.

  • Secretlab Magnus Pro XL — motorized standing desk
  • Herman Miller Aeron — with an aftermarket Atlas headrest

Homelab

Prosumer gear that mostly runs itself.

  • Ubiquiti UniFi — the whole network
  • Synology RS2423 — rackmount NAS for anything that has to persist

Woodworking

I bought into the Festool system and never looked back — safety and dust collection first.

  • SawStop PCS (3 HP, 52″) — flesh-sensing cabinet saw; the safety is the whole point
  • Harvey Gyro Air G-700 — cyclone dust collector
  • Festool Kapex — sliding miter saw
  • Festool Domino DF 500 — loose-tenon joinery
  • Festool CT 26 — dust extractor
  • Festool ETS 125 — random-orbit sander
  • Festool TS 55 — track saw for sheet goods
  • Rikon 14″ bandsaw — resawing and curves
  • DeWalt DW735X — thickness planer
  • Onefinity Woodworker X-50 — CNC router

3D Printing

Bambu, because I’d rather print than tune. Both run an AMS for multicolor.

  • Bambu Lab A1 + AMS — open-frame workhorse
  • Bambu Lab P2S + AMS — the enclosed one

Photography

Two cameras with different personalities, and a pocket gimbal for everything else.

  • Fujifilm X-T2 — dials, not menus
  • Sony a7R V — 61 MP for when detail matters
  • DJI Pocket 3 — pocket gimbal for video
